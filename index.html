Studio Protocol

Retainer & Settlement

A non-refundable retainer fee of 50% is strictly required to lock down your specific date and hour. The remaining 50% balance must be settled completely on the immediate day of your session.

Punctuality & Scheduling

To preserve our scheduling matrix, time management is paramount. Arrivals extending beyond 20 minutes late will automatically attract a static late fee of ₦15,000.

Selection & Turnaround

Unedited proofs are uploaded directly to Pixieset for your secure selection, or chosen directly at the studio post-session. Final polished media is processed within 2 to 4 working days. Unedited RAW files are strictly withheld but can be purchased upon request.

Licensing & Ownership

Clients receive a personal-use license for self-printing and digital publication. Liberty Pictures retains legal copyright ownership for archive display. Exclusive usage waivers are subject to contract negotiations.
