Guidelines

Booking & Payment Terms

A non-refundable retainer of 70% is required to secure your selected date and session time. The remaining balance of 30% is due completely on the day of the shoot. Retainers remain valid for up to 12 months under appropriate circumstances.

Rescheduling & Cancellations

We accommodate one-time session rescheduling free of charge, provided the request is submitted at least 24 to 48 hours prior to your appointment. General cancellations do not qualify for a refund. In the event of emergency cancellations without notice or no-shows, the retainer will be completely forfeited to ensure fairness to other clients and protect our studio resources.

Punctuality Policy

To ensure a smooth, unhurried, and premium creative experience, we advise all clients to be highly time-conscious. Arrivals extending past 20 minutes from the scheduled appointment time may attract a late fee of ₦10,000.

Post-Shoot Selection & Delivery

Following your session, you will be provided with an online selection gallery containing 60 to 200 of the best proof captures from the day. Alternatively, you may choose to make your final selections immediately after the session at the studio. Standard delivery turnaround ranges between 3 to 7 working days once your final selections are submitted. Proof previews can be made available for download upon request and are completely unwatermarked. Note: To preserve our signature image quality, proprietary RAW formats (such as CR2 or CR3 files) are excluded from basic delivery options.

Image Rights & Usage

Clients are granted a non-exclusive personal-use license for sharing and printing their delivered media. Liberty Pictures retains full legal copyright ownership and reserves the right to showcase selected images within our professional portfolio or promotional channels. Full commercial ownership and exclusive usage rights may be explicitly acquired via separate negotiation.
