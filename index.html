Studio Policies

Retainer & Settlement

A non-refundable retainer fee of 70% is strictly required to lock down your specific date and hour. The remaining 30% balance must be settled completely on the immediate day of your session.

Punctuality & Scheduling

To preserve our seamless calendar, sessions are time-bound: 60 minutes for 1-look and duo setups, 75 minutes for 2-look portfolios, and 90 minutes for 3-look and family configurations. Arrivals extending beyond 20 minutes late automatically attract a fee of ₦15,000.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

Cancellations do not qualify for refunds. One time rescheduling is allowed free of charge if requested at least 24-48 hours before session. Retainers are valid for 12 months.

Selection & Turnaround

Clients select their final images from an unedited JPEG gallery hosted online. Final processed files are delivered within 2 to 4 working days via a secure online gallery valid for 1 month. The original, unedited camera RAW files (the large native files straight out of the camera, not JPEGs) can be downloaded if requested and paid for.

Image Rights & Usage

Clients are granted a non-exclusive personal-use license for sharing and printing. Libertypictures retains full ownership of all images, and may share selected images for portfolio or promotional purposes.
