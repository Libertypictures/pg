Studio Protocol

Retainer & Settlement

A non-refundable retainer fee of 50% is strictly required to lock down your specific date and hour. The remaining 50% balance must be settled completely on the immediate day of your session.

Punctuality & Scheduling

To preserve our seamless studio calendar, sessions are strictly bound to their time frames: 60 minutes for 1-concept and duo setups, 75 minutes for 2-concept portfolios, and 90 minutes for 3-concept and family configurations. Arrivals extending beyond 20 minutes late automatically attract a fee of ₦15,000.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

Cancellations do not qualify for refunds. In the case of emergency cancellations or no-show retainer will be forfeited. This ensures fairness to other clients and respects our time and resources. One time rescheduling is allowed free of charge if requested at least 24-48 hours before session. Retainers are valid for 12 months under the right circumstances.

Selection & Turnaround

Unedited proofs are uploaded directly to a private proofing portal or selected at the studio post-session. A full download of unwatermarked JPEG proofs is provided for client selection. Final processed files are delivered within 2 to 4 working days, hosted via a secure online gallery valid for 1 month. Unedited RAW files are withheld but can be purchased upon request.

Licensing & Ownership

Clients receive a personal-use license for self-printing and digital publication. Libertypictures retains legal copyright ownership for archive display. Exclusive usage waivers are subject to contract negotiations.
