Guidelines

Booking & Payment Terms

A non-refundable retainer of 50% is required to secure your selected date and session time. The remaining balance of 50% is due completely on the day of the shoot. Retainers remain valid for up to 12 months under appropriate circumstances.

Rescheduling & Cancellations

We accommodate one-time session rescheduling free of charge, provided the request is submitted at least 24 to 48 hours prior to your appointment. General cancellations do not qualify for a refund. In the event of emergency cancellations without notice or no-shows, the retainer will be completely forfeited to ensure fairness to other clients and protect our studio resources.

Punctuality Policy

To ensure a smooth, unhurried, and premium experience, we advise all clients to be highly time-conscious. Arrivals extending past 20 minutes from the scheduled appointment time may attract a late fee of ₦10,000.

Post-Shoot Selection & Delivery

Following your session, unedited JPEGs will be uploaded to your personalized gallery for selection. Alternatively, you may choose to make your final selections immediately after the session at the studio. Standard delivery turnaround ranges between 3 to 7 working days once your final selections are submitted. Proof previews are completely unwatermarked. Note: Unedited RAW files are available for download upon request, subject to an additional fee.

Image Rights & Usage

Clients are granted a non-exclusive personal-use license for sharing and printing their delivered media. Liberty Pictures retains full legal copyright ownership and reserves the right to showcase selected images within our professional portfolio or promotional channels. Full commercial ownership and exclusive usage rights may be explicitly acquired via separate negotiation.
